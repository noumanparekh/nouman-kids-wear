# Nouman Kids Wear - Website Management Guide

**Welcome!** This guide will help you manage your website content easily through the Sanity Studio dashboard.

## 📍 How to Access the Dashboard

1. Open your browser and go to: **https://nouman-kids-wear.vercel.app/studio**
2. Sign in with your Google account (you'll need to be added as an admin first)
3. You'll see the main dashboard with these sections on the left sidebar

---

## 🛍️ Managing Products

### How to Add a New Product

1. Click **"Products"** in the left sidebar
2. Click the **"+"** button (top right)
3. Fill in the following details:

**Basic Product Details:**
- **Product Name**: Enter the product name (Example: Boys Ethnic Kurta Set)
- **Category**: Select from the dropdown (Example: Boys Ethnic Wear)
- **Gender**: Select Boys, Girls, or Unisex
- **Age Range Display**: Enter age range text (Example: 4–14 Years, 1×14 Years)
- **Available Sizes**: Enter size information (Example: 1×14, 4×10, 16×40, 24×40)
- **Product Colors**: Select all colors available for this product
- **Price Text**: Enter pricing info (Example: Price on Request, ₹499 onwards)

**Images:**
- **Product Images**: Click to upload photos (you can add multiple photos)
- **Image Alt Text**: Describe what's in each photo (optional but recommended)

**Website Display:**
- **Badge**: Optional text to show on product card (Example: Best Seller, Limited Stock)
- **New Arrival**: ✅ Turn ON to show in "New Arrivals" section
- **Featured Product**: ✅ Turn ON to highlight on homepage
- **Stock Status**: Select In Stock, Low Stock, or Out of Stock
- **Show on Website**: ✅ Keep ON to make product visible (turn OFF to hide)
- **Display Order**: Lower numbers appear first (Example: 1, 2, 3)

**Advanced/SEO (collapsed by default):**
- **Slug**: Click "Generate" to auto-create from product name (you don't need to change this)

4. Click **"Publish"** button (bottom right) to save

---

### How to Mark a Product as "New Arrival"

1. Open the product you want to mark as new
2. Scroll to **"Website Display"** section
3. Turn ON the toggle: **"✅ New Arrival"**
4. Click **"Publish"**

The product will now appear in the "New Arrivals" section on your homepage.

---

### How to Hide a Product (Without Deleting)

1. Open the product you want to hide
2. Scroll to **"Website Display"** section
3. Turn OFF the toggle: **"✅ Show on Website"**
4. Click **"Publish"**

The product is now hidden from your website but still saved in the dashboard. You can turn it back ON anytime.

**Why hide instead of delete?**
- Hiding is temporary — you can bring the product back anytime
- Useful for seasonal items or out-of-stock products
- No data is lost — all photos and details are preserved

---

### How to Permanently Remove a Product

⚠️ **Warning:** Deleting is permanent and cannot be undone!

1. Open the product you want to delete
2. Click the **three dots menu** (•••) in the top right
3. Select **"Delete"**
4. Confirm deletion

**Before deleting, consider:**
- Can you hide it instead by turning OFF "Show on Website"?
- Do you have the product photos saved elsewhere?
- Once deleted, you'll need to recreate the entire product entry

---

### How to Remove a Product from "New Arrivals"

1. Open the product
2. Scroll to **"Website Display"** section
3. Turn OFF the toggle: **"✅ New Arrival"**
4. Click **"Publish"**

The product stays on the website but no longer appears in the "New Arrivals" section.

---

### How to Remove a Product from Homepage Highlights

1. Open the product
2. Scroll to **"Website Display"** section
3. Turn OFF the toggle: **"✅ Featured Product"**
4. Click **"Publish"**

The product stays in the catalogue but is no longer highlighted on the homepage.

---

### How to Upload Product Photos

1. Open the product
2. In the **"Images"** section, click **"Upload"**
3. Select photo(s) from your computer
4. Wait for upload to complete (you'll see a thumbnail)
5. Add **"Image Alt Text"** (describe what's in the photo)
6. Click **"Publish"**

**Photo Tips:**
- Use clear, well-lit photos
- Show the product from different angles
- Use consistent background if possible
- Recommended size: at least 800x1000 pixels

## 🏪 Managing Store Information

1. Click **"Store Information"** in the left sidebar
2. There is only ONE store info document—click to open it
3. Update any of these fields:

**Contact Details:**
- Store Name
- Phone Number (with country code, Example: +91 9876543210)
- WhatsApp Number (same format as phone)
- Email Address

**Address:**
- Street Address
- City
- State
- Postal Code
- Google Maps Link (paste the share link from Google Maps)

**Store Hours:**
- Opening Time (Example: 10:00 AM)
- Closing Time (Example: 8:00 PM)
- Open Days (Example: Monday to Saturday)
- Weekly Holiday (Example: Sunday)

**Social Media:**
- Instagram URL (paste your full Instagram profile link)
- Facebook URL (paste your full Facebook page link)

4. Click **"Publish"** to save changes

**⚠️ Important:** 
- Do NOT create a new store info document. Only edit the existing one.
- Changes to phone/WhatsApp numbers update throughout the entire website
- Address changes affect the contact section and footer

---

### How to Update Store Phone Number

This updates the phone number shown everywhere on the website (header, contact section, footer).

1. Click **"Store Information"** in sidebar
2. Update **Phone Number** field (Example: +91 9876543210)
3. Update **WhatsApp Number** if different
4. Click **"Publish"**

The new number appears on the website within 1-2 minutes.

---

### How to Change Store Address

1. Click **"Store Information"** in sidebar
2. Update address fields:
   - **Street Address**: Your shop's street address
   - **City**: Adilabad
   - **State**: Telangana
   - **Postal Code**: Your pincode
3. If you have a Google Maps link, paste it in **Google Maps Link**
4. Click **"Publish"**

---

### How to Update Store Timings

1. Click **"Store Information"** in sidebar
2. Update **Store Hours** fields:
   - **Opening Time**: When you open (Example: 10:00 AM)
   - **Closing Time**: When you close (Example: 8:00 PM)

## 🎯 Managing Homepage Banner

1. Click **"Homepage Banner"** in the left sidebar
2. There is only ONE banner document—click to open it
3. Update these fields:

- **Main Heading**: The big title text (Example: Latest Kids Fashion)
- **Subtitle**: Short description below the heading
- **Background Image**: Upload a banner image (desktop)
- **Mobile Background Image**: Optional separate image for mobile
- **Button Text**: Text on the button (Example: Shop Now, View Products)
- **Button Link**: Where button goes (Example: #products, #catalogue)
- **Show Banner**: ✅ Turn ON to display, OFF to hide

4. Click **"Publish"** to save

**⚠️ Important:** Do NOT create a new banner. Only edit the existing one.

---

### How to Change Homepage Banner Image

1. Click **"Homepage Banner"** in sidebar
2. Click on **Background Image** field
3. Click **"Upload"** or select from media library
4. Wait for upload to complete
5. Optionally upload a **Mobile Background Image** (different image for phones)
6. Click **"Publish"**

**Image Tips:**
- Desktop banner: at least 1920x600 pixels (wide landscape)
- Mobile banner: at least 800x1000 pixels (portrait or square)
- Use clear, bright photos that show your products or store

---

### How to Update Homepage Heading

1. Click **"Homepage Banner"** in sidebar
2. Update **Main Heading** (Example: "Latest Kids Fashion" or "Premium Kidswear Collection")
3. Update **Subtitle** (1-2 lines describing your store)
4. Click **"Publish"**

The heading appears prominently on the homepage hero section.

---

### How to Temporarily Hide the Banner

1. Click **"Homepage Banner"** in sidebar
2. Turn OFF **Show Banner** toggle
3. Click **"Publish"**

The banner is now hidden. Website will show default hero content instead. Turn it back ON anytime.

## 📂 Managing Categories

Categories are preset groups like "Boys Ethnic Wear", "Girls Party Wear", etc.

### How to Edit a Category

1. Click **"Categories"** in the left sidebar
2. Click on the category you want to edit
3. You can update:
   - **Category Name**
   - **Short Description**
   - **Category Image**
   - **UI Accent Color** (used for website styling, NOT product color)
   - **Display Order** (lower numbers appear first)
   - **Show on Website** (turn OFF to hide)

4. Click **"Publish"** to save

**Note:** Category "Accent Color" is ONLY for website styling. Actual product colors are set in the product itself.

---

## 🖼️ Managing Gallery Photos

### How to Add a Gallery Photo

1. Click **"Gallery"** in the left sidebar
2. Click the **"+"** button
3. Fill in:
   - **Photo Title**: Descriptive name (Example: Store Front View, Ethnic Wear Section)
   - **Upload Photo**: Click to upload your photo
   - **Image Description**: Describe what's in the photo (optional)
   - **Photo Category**: Select type (Store Interior, Store Exterior, Product Display, Events, Other)
   - **Display Order**: Lower numbers appear first
   - **Show in Gallery**: ✅ Keep ON to display

4. Click **"Publish"** to save

---

### How to Remove or Hide a Gallery Photo

**To hide temporarily:**
1. Open the gallery photo
2. Turn OFF **Show in Gallery** toggle
3. Click **"Publish"**

Photo is hidden but not deleted. Turn it back ON anytime.

**To permanently delete:**
1. Open the gallery photo
2. Click **three dots menu** (•••) in top right
3. Select **"Delete"**
4. Confirm deletion

⚠️ Deletion is permanent. Consider hiding instead if you might want it back later.

---

### How to Reorder Gallery Photos

Gallery photos appear in the order set by **Display Order** number.

1. Open each photo you want to reorder
2. Change the **Display Order** number
   - Lower numbers (1, 2, 3) appear first
   - Higher numbers (10, 11, 12) appear last
3. Click **"Publish"** for each photo

**Example:**
- First photo: Display Order = 1
- Second photo: Display Order = 2
- Third photo: Display Order = 3

## 🚨 Important Things to Remember

### ✅ DO:
- Always click **"Publish"** after making changes (changes won't show on website until published)
- Use clear, descriptive names for products and photos
- Upload high-quality product photos
- Keep product names short and clear
- Mark products as "New Arrival" to highlight them
- Use "Display Order" to control what appears first

### ❌ DON'T:
- DON'T create multiple "Store Information" documents (only edit the existing one)
- DON'T create multiple "Homepage Banner" documents (only edit the existing one)
- DON'T delete products—turn OFF "Show on Website" instead to hide them
- DON'T manually edit the "Slug" field unless necessary (it auto-generates)
- DON'T worry if something goes wrong—you can always undo or re-edit

## 🆘 Common Questions

**Q: How long until my changes appear on the website?**  
A: Changes appear within 1-2 minutes after clicking "Publish"

**Q: I want to temporarily remove a product but not delete it**  
A: Open the product and turn OFF "Show on Website" toggle, then Publish

**Q: Can I upload multiple product photos?**  
A: Yes! In the "Images" section, you can add as many photos as you want

**Q: What size should product photos be?**  
A: At least 800x1000 pixels recommended. Sanity will automatically optimize them

**Q: How do I change the phone number on the website?**  
A: Edit "Store Information" → Contact Details → Phone Number → Publish

**Q: What is a "Slug"?**  
A: It's the URL-friendly version of your product/category name. Always click "Generate" and don't edit it manually

**Q: What is "Accent Color" vs "Product Colors"?**  
A: **Accent Color** is for website styling (buttons, badges). **Product Colors** are the actual colors your product comes in (black, mint green, etc.)
